Pizza

Description of the Pizza


A pizza is an italian dish that has existed for many generations.

The pizza is usally topped with tomatoes, olvies, pizza sauce etc:

Description

Ingredients for dough only

  1. 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water
  2. 1 Package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active yeast
  3. 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  4. 2 tablespoons extra virigin olive oil
  5. 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  6. 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps for making the dough

  • Proof the yeast by placing it in a bowl with warm water for 5 minutes
  • Add flour, salt, sugar and olives. Then, Knead the pizza dough
  • Let the dough rise and cover with plastic wrap
